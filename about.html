About Us


BackWoods University is a university for ambitious students who want to reach their full potential and make their dreams a reality.
Founded in 1987, BackWoods University has grown to become a renowned institution, renowned for its commitment to excellence in education and its welcoming, friendly atmosphere.

Our aim is to provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that encourages our students to reach their full potential - both academically and personally. We offer a wide range of courses, to ensure that our students are best equipped to succeed in the modern market.
At BackWoods University, we believe in the power of education. We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background and circumstances. That's why we offer a range of scholarships, bursaries and other support for students who need financial assistance. We are also committed to providing students with the best possible experience, from the moment they begin their studies until they graduate.

BackWoods University is proud to be a leader in technology-enabled learning. We are always on the lookout for new, innovative ways to help our students learn, whether that's through online resources or other digital tools. We also have a range of physical resources, such as our library and learning centre, which provide our students with access to all the materials they need to succeed. Above all, BackWoods University is a place where ambition, innovation and success go hand in hand. Our staff and faculties are dedicated to helping our students reach their goals and fulfill their ambitions. We also have a strong network of alumni, who are always ready to provide support and guidance to our current students.
We invite all prospective students to contact us to find out more about our courses and how we can help you reach your full potential - whatever that may be!
